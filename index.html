From the Vice Chancellor's Desk
“Our mission is simple — equip every student who walks through Renala Khurd's gates with the knowledge, character and confidence to lead. Education here is not a transaction; it is a trust we carry for the families and communities who send their children to us.”

University of Okara was established in 2016 with a clear purpose: bring affordable, internationally-aligned higher education to Okara and the surrounding districts. Today our faculty and students work side by side across computing, sciences, social sciences, life sciences, health sciences, education and agriculture — chasing research, service and skill in equal measure.

— Office of the Vice Chancellor
