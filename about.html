About University of Okara

Our history, mission and the people behind a growing public university.

Our Story

From a campus to a charter

The roots of University of Okara reach back to 2005, when the Okara campus of the University of Education, Lahore, first opened its doors with a single department of Chemistry. What began as one science department grew steadily — Mathematics joined the following year, and the campus kept expanding its academic footprint through the 2010s.

On 23 February 2016, the Provincial Assembly of the Punjab passed the University of Okara Bill, and on 1 April 2016 the Government of Punjab notified the upgrade — Okara campus became University of Okara, a full, independent public-sector university.

2005

Okara campus of the University of Education, Lahore inaugurated with the Department of Chemistry — the institution's first science department.

2006 – 2014

Mathematics, Education, Islamic Studies, Zoology and further departments launch; postgraduate programs begin in select disciplines.

2016

University of Okara Bill passed by the Punjab Assembly; campus formally upgraded to an independent, chartered public university.

Today

Seven faculties, 60+ departments, two campuses (Main Campus, Renala Khurd & City Campus, Okara) and a growing research footprint.

At a Glance

  • Main Campus — GT Road, Renala Khurd (≈205 acres); City Campus — Samadpura Road, Okara
  • Recognized by the Higher Education Commission (HEC) of Pakistan
  • Signatory to the Magna Charta Universitatum
  • 7 Faculties · 60+ Departments · Undergraduate to PhD

Mission & Vision

Four commitments that guide every faculty, department and decision at University of Okara.

Quality Education

Internationally-aligned, HEC-recognized programs taught by qualified, full-time faculty.

Applied Research

Research that responds to real problems in agriculture, health, technology and society.

Inclusive Community

A diverse student body drawn from Okara and the wider Punjab region, regardless of background.

Service & Ethics

Graduates equipped to lead with integrity — true to our motto, "Enter to Learn, Leave to Serve."

A Message From The Vice Chancellor
“As Vice Chancellor, I extend a warm welcome to every admission aspirant of University of Okara. We are a public-sector institution accredited by the Higher Education Commission, committed to giving each student a contemporary, well-supervised academic experience — across Engineering, Information Technology, Sciences and Social Sciences alike.”

Our faculty dedicates itself to close, diligent guidance throughout every student's course of study. Equipping students to participate actively in society, and to excel as responsible citizens and leaders in an increasingly complex world, remains our central charge — delivered through a comprehensive educational experience spanning Engineering, IT, Sciences and Social Sciences. A newly-established Skill Development Centre further extends this mission with hands-on training for both fresh graduates and working professionals.

— Office of the Vice Chancellor, University of Okara
